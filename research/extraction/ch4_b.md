- **Beats:**
  - Hollow 10-ft stone globe (1-ft-thick shell) floats 5 ft up; depicts Golarion 10,000+ years ago; strong divination aura
  - Touch + concentrate on stars/sky = teleported inside into suspended animation, mind cast into space visions
  - Exit anytime by concentrating on Golarion/anything on it; auto-shunted out at Int 0
  - Magic has degraded — now mostly madness-inducing visions (the Will save / Int damage above)
- **Respect Points:** gain 3 if PCs gain the Mark in front of a Shoanti NPC or display/manifest it before one
- **Story Award:** 9,600 XP if at least one PC gains the Thrallkeeper's Mark

### Trial of the Totem / Raising the Totems (lines 9562, 9574)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** none
- **Encounter:** none (endurance challenge; see Hungry Visitors below)
- **DCs / mechanics:**
  - Sun totems: fired-clay pylons, 10 ft tall, hollow, 500 lbs each; hardness 6, hp 40; glow at night (visible from Flameford)
  - Carrying: Str 10 can barely drag one; Str 17+ can lift it and stagger at speed 5 ft; teamwork allowed with no penalty; anyone who can drag 500 lbs can erect one
  - Day 1: 1 hour to carry totems up to the lower tier (30-ft plateau) of Bolt Rock and erect them in depressions; keep upright 1 day. Sunrise day 2: 1 hour to move all totems to the highest mesa (40 ft); keep upright another day. Third sunrise: standing totem = Sklar-Quah citizenship for that PC
  - Holding upright: DC 12 Strength check every 6 hours (can take 10); failure = totem leans, DC 16 Strength to stabilize; second failure = totem topples, takes 3d6 damage; a shattered totem permanently disqualifies that character
  - Exhaustion: hot conditions may force Fortitude saves vs. nonlethal damage (Core Rulebook 444); day 2 with no sleep = fatigued; lesser restoration / paladin mercies etc. are legal
  - Thirst: standard rules (1 day + Con score in hours; then Con check each hour, DC 10 +1 per previous check, or 1d6 nonlethal; nonlethal = fatigued); short 5-10-minute food/water breaks on Bolt Rock OK
  - Magic: endure elements / create food and water acceptable; stone shape-style fixing is cheating — Sun Shaman inspects afterward and auto-notices unless disguised with a DC 20 Survival check per altered totem/site; each detected alteration = disqualified; 3+ immovable rods can hold a totem with no detectable signs
- **Treasure:** none
- **Beats:**
  - Bolt Rock: small mesa 20-min walk east of Flameford; lower plateau 30 ft up, upper mesa 40 ft; iron ore attracts lightning during storms (sacred to Sun Shamans)
  - No food/water provided for the 2 days, but PCs may bring their own and use any magic that doesn't alter totems or Bolt Rock
  - Krojun's Acknowledgement (line 9598): morning of day 2, Krojun returns calmed, climbs up to watch, grudgingly calls the PCs lucky to have each other as nalharests (siblings)
