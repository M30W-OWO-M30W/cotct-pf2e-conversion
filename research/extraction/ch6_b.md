### A37 Mess Hall (CR 14) (line 15291)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** "Four stone pillars support the ceiling of this" (read-aloud begins just before chunk start; cooking hippogriff on fire, barbs/quills on tables)

  - Three barbed-devil brothers (Frentes, Jhaun, Peveer) — damned souls promoted to hamatulas by Sermignatto; no real memories of mortal life

### A44 Seneschal's Apartment (CR 15) (line 15375)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** "This room contains a canopied bed, a desk, a"
- **Encounter:** Togomor (CR 15, human wizard 7/bloatmage 9) + Pudgyknuckles (imp familiar, hp 73)
- **DCs:**
  - DC 26 Perception: find secret wall panel above bed (Togomor's treasure cache)
  - DC 30 Knowledge (planes): identify the wormlike three-faced creature in Togomor's sketches as a belier devil
- **Treasure:** Behind secret panel: pouch of 70 pp; scroll of greater teleport; all of Togomor's spellbooks (all prepared spells + 2d6 additional spells per spell level of GM's choice, including arcane sight, darkvision, see invisibility, tongues, contingency, permanency, all three planar binding spells, and the akaruzug-creation spells — enervation, magic jar, limited wish [to duplicate unhallow]). Plus Togomor's carried gear (see stat block).
- **Beats:**
  - Room (formerly Neolandus Kalepopolis's, high standard) now squalid; continual flame in the plant-shaped lamp's amber globe lights it at all times
  - Togomor found here gorging on the bed if not encountered elsewhere; possessed by Sermignatto for years, used as a "public face"
  - Pudgyknuckles roams castle as rat (halls) or raven (battlements); fetches food; returns to Togomor when alarm raised
  - Spellbook notes cover hemotheurgy, construct creation (incl. akaruzug), rejuvenating blood baths, immortality routes; indicate Ileosa plans to use blood for immortality but hasn't told Togomor how
  - Sketchbooks full of unfinished three-faced worm drawings — manifestation of Sermignatto's possession (overwrote Togomor's escape notes)
  - Development: if slain, Togomor smiles and dies saying thanks (freed from possession). If possession is removed without killing him, he surrenders, reveals Sermignatto's history/strengths/weaknesses (A59), confirms Ileosa's devil contract (erinyes bound to her soul) is kept in A90, and explains entry via the phase door in A77. After Sermignatto dies he retrieves his spellbooks (if PCs lack them) and flees Korvosa for good.
- **Tactics/morale:** see Togomor stat block

Morale: at 30 hp or fewer, dimension door to A59 to recover.

### A52 Throne Room (CR 15) (line 15655)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** "This magnificent room is lavishly decorated with frescoes,"
- **Encounter:** False Ileosa (CR 9) + Vavana Dhatri (CR 9) + 3x Nessian Hell Hound (CR 9, XP 6,400 each, hp 114 each; Bestiary 173) + 3x Gray Maiden Palace Guard (CR 8, XP 4,800 each, hp 99 each)
- **DCs:**
  - DC 27 Spellcraft: when false Ileosa melts into blood at 0 hp, recognize she was some variant simulacrum (full details only after studying the Everdawn Pool)
  - DC 40 Spellcraft: notice the Redirection effect when casting divinations at the real Ileosa (see False Ileosa stat block)
  - DC 25 Will: negate the Crimson Throne curse (non-royal sitter; new save each round seated)
- **Treasure:**
  - 6 tapestries, 1,000 gp each
  - Crimson Throne — art object worth 10,000 gp (buyer hard to find; looting it makes PCs look like traitors to Korvosans)
- **Beats:**
  - Real Ileosa is absent (Sunken Queen); only Vavana Dhatri and Sermignatto know. The "queen" here is a simulacrum made from Ileosa's blood and Everdawn Pool magic
  - Simulacrum avoids extended contact with Red Mantis/Togomor/Gray Maidens; mostly sits alone in the royal suite (A57-A59); relocates to the throne on alarm
  - Greets PCs warmly, then coldly offers exile-or-execution ultimatum; if allowed to finish speaking, weaves suggestion via bardic performance at the party's strongest fighter (drop weapons, leave Korvosa)
  - Sabina expected to have defected to the PCs by now (or soon after this encounter); Vavana commands in her place with 3 guards and 3 Nessian hell hounds
  - Crimson Throne enchantment (not a magic item; devil-granted effect): royal bloodline (incl. simulacra) seated gains +10 deflection AC + immunity to illusions and mind-affecting effects; anyone else seated is cursed — duplicates robe of powerlessness (Core Rulebook 542), DC 25 Will negates, re-save each round seated. Removed by break enchantment, dispel evil, or remove curse (NOT dispel magic); once removed, throne is permanently nonmagical. The legendary "curse of the Crimson Throne" itself is just a legend.
- **Tactics/morale:** see stat blocks. Hell hounds: bound by Togomor's planar binding; engage on the false Ileosa's command; aim breath weapons to avoid simulacrum/Gray Maidens; fight to the death. Gray Maidens: hold position in front of the throne, block melee access to the false Ileosa; lose a round of actions in shock if she melts; fight to the death.
