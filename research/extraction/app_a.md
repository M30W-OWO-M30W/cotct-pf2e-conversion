### Korvosa's History (line 17328)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** none
- **Encounter:** none
- **DCs:** none
- **Treasure:** none
- **Beats (timeline data):**
  - Pre-colonial Shoanti guarded the Grand Mastaba (oral history: contains a great evil)
  - 4406 AR: city charter created by Emperor Halleck IV of Cheliax (247 amendments today)
  - 4407 AR: Field Marshal Jakthion Korvosa invades via Bloodsworn Vale; reinforced by Montlarion Jeggare's expedition; Shoanti driven to the Cinderlands; Fort Korvosa founded
  - 13 Neth 4429 AR: fire burns half the city; Sable Company rises as defender
  - 4462 AR: Galstak Sevendeaths (resurrected six times) unites Sklar-Quah and Skoan-Quah, besieges fort for a week; 26 years of war until his final death in 4488 AR
  - Next 12 years population nearly doubles to ~8,000; 4502 AR: Cousins' War (internal noble strife), ended with Chelish arbiters; "Fort" dropped from name
  - 4606 AR: Age of Lost Omens; Korvosa becomes independent; traditionalists leave to found Magnimar
  - 4624 AR: Eodred (son of Lord Arbust Arabasti) takes leadership; 4633 AR: title changed to king; Crimson Throne commissioned (curse: monarchs bear no heirs after coronation and die of unnatural causes)

### Korvosa Settlement Modifier Rules (line 17374)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** none
- **DCs/rules (verbatim mechanics):**
  - Corruption: modifies Bluff vs. city officials/guards and Stealth outside on streets/alleys/Shingles
  - Crime: modifies Sense Motive to avoid being bluffed and Sleight of Hand to pick pockets
  - Economy: modifies Craft, Perform, Profession checks to generate income
  - Law: modifies Intimidate to make an opponent friendly and Diplomacy vs. government officials
  - Lore: modifies Diplomacy to gather information/rumors and Knowledge checks using city resources
  - Society: modifies Disguise and Diplomacy to alter attitude of non-official citizens
  - Danger: modifies all random-encounter rolls in Korvosa (incl. sewers and Shingles); encounter tables at book p.465
- **Beats:**
  - City stat block shifts between 5 states as the campaign progresses (baseline -> Unrest/Plagued/Martial Law/Anarchy per chapter triggers)
  - Variant stat blocks keep baseline population and item lists

### Home and Hearth / Inn rules (line 17985)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** none
- **DCs/rules:**
  - Luxurious services: double Core Rulebook prices; no chance of dangerous encounter
  - Standard services: normal prices; 0% base encounter chance, modified by Korvosa's Danger rating during anarchy/martial law/plague/unrest
  - Squalid services: half prices; 20% base encounter chance (also Danger-modified)
  - Check d% once per visit; on an encounter roll on Korvosa Street Encounters table (p.465); 75% (standard) / 25% (squalid) chance the encounter is outside on the street; improbable results = no encounter; may downgrade to a belligerent-drunk confrontation
- **Beats:**
  - Slum living costs a few coppers/day; affluent areas dozens of gp/day; use Core Rulebook p.405 cost-of-living rules
  - PCs may use Citadel Volshyenek early (Guard allies) but later need hidden HQs as the queen hunts them
