- Init +2; Senses darkvision 60 ft.; Perception +5
- AC 13, touch 13, flat-footed 11 (+2 Dex, +1 size)
- hp 5 (1d8+1)
- Fort +3, Ref +2, Will +1
- Immune mind-affecting effects
- Speed 30 ft., climb 20 ft.
- Melee bite +1 (1d4 plus poison)
- Special Attacks dream web, poison, web (+3 ranged, DC 11, 1 hp)
- Str 10, Dex 15, Con 12, Int —, Wis 12, Cha 11
- Base Atk +0; CMB -1; CMD 11
- Skills Climb +8, Perception +5, Stealth +6 (+10 in its webs); Racial Modifiers +4 Perception, +4 Stealth in its webs
- Ecology: Environment tropical forest or any urban; Organization solitary, pair, or colony (3-8); Treasure incidental
- Special Abilities:
  - **Dream Web (Ex):** iridescent webs, easier to spot (Perception DC 15); animals/humanoids/monstrous humanoids contacting webs hallucinate — 1 Wisdom damage per round of contact, DC 11 Fort negates. Burned webs release poison gas in 10-ft radius: 1d4 Wisdom damage to all in area, DC 11 Fort half; vapor lasts 1 round. Wisdom damage is a poison effect. DC Con-based.
  - **Poison (Ex):** Bite—injury; Fort DC 11; frequency 1/round for 4 rounds; effect 1d2 Wis; cure 1 save.
- Lore (paraphrase): Mwangi transplants infesting the Shingles; venom is distilled into the drug shiver.
