### D. Arkona Palace — location intro (line 6652)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** none
- **Encounter:** none (servants/guards in outbuildings are 1st- or 2nd-level human experts/warriors, play no role)
- **DCs:** DC 30 Knowledge (religion): identify Chamidu (Vudrani God of Wild Beasts, six-armed four-faced giant riding a tiger with human hands for paws) on the carved mahogany doors

  - Palace atop Endrin Isle, highest point of Old Korvosa; Vudrani style; only Arkonas live inside

### Securing an Audience (line 6658)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** none
- **Encounter:** none (social)
- **DCs:** none
- **Treasure:** none
- **Beats:**
  - Daytime visitors intercepted by patrol of 6 house guards; believable request (e.g., audience with Glorio) gets escort to D1
  - Carnochan (eye-patched majordomo) greets; has permanent telepathic bond with Bahor, alerts him with descriptions on arrival
  - PCs wait in D2; Bahor observes them via clairaudience/clairvoyance through a third eye bloodstone; if PCs wander off instead, palace goes on alert

  - Demands quid pro quo: PCs assassinate the Emperor of Old Korvosa (Pilts); in return he'll help them find their friends; if Pilts already dead, proceeds straight to Bahor's Offer
  - If PCs haven't killed Pilts, they're escorted out and told not to return until it's done

### D4 Garden (CR 8 or 11) (line 6874)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** "This chamber hardly seems to be part of"
- **Encounter:** base — Marble Elephant (CR 7) + Large Water Elemental (CR 5); on alert add Avishandu and Nudhaali, 2x Marai Rakshasa CR 8 (CR 11 total)

  - DC 15 Perception: distinguish balcony edge (25 ft up) from walls/dome when looking up from ground level (easier if a creature stands visibly on it)
  - DC 25 Perception: find circular secret trap door (10-ft-diameter stone platform under elephant statue)

  - DC 30 Disable Device: unlock either set of double doors to the grounds
- **Treasure:**
  - Two bloodstones in fountain-cobra mouths, 100 gp each; the west-facing one is a third eye bloodstone
  - Variant marble elephant figurine of wondrous power worth 26,000 gp (extra power: can become a life-sized marble statue as per statue spell; assuming statue form counts as one monthly activation but lasts indefinitely)
- **Beats:**
  - Indoor Vudrani jungle: illusion-enhanced wall paintings, fake fence/gate-doors; live birds and small animals; magically maintained temperature/humidity
  - Windows and skylights bear permanent silent image so the illusion isn't broken; large glass dome over north portion
  - Hidden 6-inch pressure plate at platform center rotates the disk 180 degrees, opening spiral stairs down to D19; stays open 10 minutes then auto-closes; pressure plates also close it manually
  - Passphrase "Chamidu is blind" — anyone using the trap door without speaking it activates the elephant
  - Water elemental animates the fountain plume; ignores intruders unless they open the secret trap door or it sees the Arkonas attack them; pursues only within this chamber, fights to the death
  - Elephant attacks trap-door users, returns to pedestal when door closes; reverts to figurine if slain
  - Double doors to grounds: locked, magic mouth alarms like D1 but no bloodstones
- **Tactics/morale:** on alert, Avishandu + Nudhaali hide in the howdah; pre-cast mage armor, then invisibility (recast once after 5 min; abandon post after second 5-minute period to join patrol). In combat Avishandu activates the elephant and directs it at PCs; Nudhaali casts scorching ray. They stay human and cast spells until exposed as rakshasas, then revert to true form and use energy bolts.

### E. The Vivified Labyrinth — intro and gear mechanics (line 7320; PARTIAL — continues in next chunk)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** none
- **Encounter:** roaming foes Vimanda (CR 12) and the darksphinx Sivit, presented separately from rooms
- **DCs / mechanics:**
  - Four circular dungeon sections built inside giant gears; levers rotate them one-quarter turn (direction per map arrows)
  - Five levers total (one hidden); a pulled lever locks for 1 minute; DC 30 Disable Device (full-round action) unlocks a lever early for an extra use
  - All four sections rotate simultaneously, taking 1 round per 90 degrees; openings close rapidly
  - Creature in a transitional square: DC 15 Reflex — success: adjust 5 ft either direction out of the square; failure: scissored by walls for 10d6 damage and forced into a random nontransitional adjacent square
  - Creature standing in a rotating gear area: DC 10 Acrobatics or fall prone
  - Grinding noise plainly audible throughout areas E1-E20
  - Ceilings 10 ft unless specified; rooms unlit
  - Dungeon has four orientations; only the mapped first orientation allows entry from E2 without teleportation
- **Beats:**
  - Built for Arkona entertainment: training/testing recruits, punishing prisoners
  - Bahor's favorite game: prisoner with knife and loincloth, slow venom, promised a curing pool deep inside — no survivors so far

### The Revolving Dungeon — GM tips (line 7344)
- Tip 1: roll initiative when PCs trigger the first rotation in E3; roll for Sivit and Vimanda too and track their movement secretly each of their turns
- Tip 2: prepare four 8-inch paper circles with grids; sketch dungeon onto them so each can physically rotate 90 degrees
- Tip 3: if PCs get trapped away from all levers, have an NPC elsewhere pull one
- Tip 4: optionally drop rotation entirely — connect E3/E4, E14/E15, and E17/E18 with tunnels
