### A. VENCARLO'S HOME (CR 10) (line 5796)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** none (no boxed text for the area overview)
- **Encounter:** 2x Red Mantis Assassin CR 8 (overall encounter CR 10)
- **DCs:**
  - DC 20 Perception (once PCs reach A7 or A8): notice smoke scent with a strange tang; +10 circumstance bonus with scent ability
  - DC 15 Craft (alchemy): identify the odor as residual alchemist's fire
  - DC 25 Perception: walls/floor/furniture faintly stained with alchemist's fire in multiple places
  - DC 20 Survival: building unused for some time (since around Kroft's last letter)
  - DC 30 Survival: dust smears/fresh scratches — someone very stealthy has been inside (Red Mantis traces)
  - DC 30 Perception (A8): hidden panel in east closet wall
  - DC 40 Disable Device: open the iron lockbox (or hardness 10, hp 40, break DC 23; key is with Vencarlo's confiscated gear, Arkona Palace area D16, p171)
  - DC 15 Knowledge (local): recognize Blackjack's signature gear (automatic for PCs who attended Trinia's rescued execution in Ch1)
  - DC 25 Perception: find the iron coffer in the rubble if the house burns (contents unharmed)
  - Fire hazard: each assassin throws alchemist's fire at the fireplace on her floor; 3 fireplace squares burn; spreads 1d4 adjacent squares/round; burning square = 1d6 fire damage + DC 15 Reflex or catch fire
- **Treasure:** Hidden Blackjack cache (A8 closet, in iron lockbox): bag of holding (type I) containing — black hooded cloak of elvenkind; several black masks; a dozen masterwork daggers with stylized "B" engraved on pommels; black leather suit of +2 slick leather armor; black leather boots of elvenkind; amulet of proof against detection and location; black leather gloves of swimming and climbing (two fingers of the right hand contain fake, adjustable wooden fingers); +2 keen mithral rapier. (No gp values printed.)
- **Beats:**
  - Humble house east of the burned-down Orisini Academy (Red Mantis arson); door closed, unlocked; interior warm, quiet, unlit; knocks/calls unanswered.
  - Vencarlo absent since the academy burned (captured by Arkonas); 2 Red Mantis assassins squat here on 8-hour shifts (relieved by pairs from the hidden HQ below the Longacre Building, Ch4) hoping to ambush a returning Vencarlo.
  - Ambush positions: one behind the northern sofa nook in A6 (under the stairs), one in the rafters above A7 (14-ft ceiling); on hearing entry, they spend mask charges (+5 Perception, see invisibility, deathwatch — or darkvision at night) and run prep magic.
  - Blackjack legacy: cache lets a PC become the next Blackjack; the identity has passed hero-to-hero for generations; Vencarlo is the current one.
  - Development: if the fire isn't doused, the house burns down (locals + Arkona brigades contain the blaze but can't save the house).
  - Room key: A1 Entrance (Vudrani throw rug); A2 Workshop (blade repair, partially repaired daggers/rapiers, nothing valuable); A3 Bathroom (tub, toilet); A4 Study (desk, 2 book cabinets — swordplay/philosophy; ledgers show academy business collapsed after Eodred II died); A5 Pantry (spoiled perishables; no kitchen); A6 Living Room (2 sofas, lit fireplace despite "abandoned" house); A7 Training Room (practice dummies, lit fireplace, 14-ft ceiling, exposed rafters); A8 Bedroom (lived-in but unslept bed, desk, chair, closet with hidden panel).
- **Tactics/morale:** Before combat each assassin drinks potion of resist fire + potion of bull's strength, casts magic weapon on blades, activates all mask abilities; stay hidden until spotted or a PC climbs to A7. Round 1: each throws alchemist's fire at her floor's fireplace to ignite the room. They are fire-proof while potions last and exploit the flames; activate red shroud and converge to flank. If only one PC is visible at combat start, an assassin tries a prayer attack on that PC before lighting the fire. Fight to the death.
