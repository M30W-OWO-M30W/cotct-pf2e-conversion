### EVENT 1: Korvosa's New Hero (CR 15) (line 14535)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** "Greetings, heroes of yesterday! My name is Trifaccia. Have you"
- **Encounter:** Yzahnum, male efreeti fighter 4/rogue 7, CR 15 (as faux hero "Trifaccia"); 12x False Rebels (Charlatans, NPC Codex 145), CR 3 each
- **DCs:**
  - Will DC 22: negate *plane shift* wish ("I wish you'd go to Hell!" — random PC sent to Hell on failure)
  - Fortitude DC 22: negate *baleful polymorph* wish (random PC becomes a small pig)
  - Fortitude DC 22: *finger of death* wish (random PC)
  - All wish effects function at CL 12th
- **Treasure:** (see Yzahnum gear in stat block)
- **Beats:**
  - The efreeti Yzahnum (formerly crafting Gray Maiden arms) poses as masked hero "Trifaccia"; staged "rebels" (paid Chelish thugs) rob citizens, Trifaccia "saves" them and hands out coin — propaganda to discredit the real rebels
  - Trigger: occurs anywhere in Korvosa after the PCs do something publicly obvious (e.g., kill a Gray Maiden patrol or first castle foray)
  - ~12 thugs surround and taunt the PCs, voicing "wishes"; hidden Yzahnum grants up to three of them as hostile spell effects with no obvious source
  - After 3 wishes / thugs beaten / efreeti spotted: Trifaccia steps out (slender man, black clothes, brass mask, golden flaming scimitar), thugs flee crying his name
  - He challenges one PC to a duel, loser's party leaves Korvosa forever; goal is to humiliate the PC or bait allies into interfering before a crowd
  - Weakness: exposing his true efreeti nature (antimagic field, enchantment compulsion, etc.) destroys the Trifaccia persona — worth 1 RP
- **Tactics/morale:** As Trifaccia (Medium form, AC 1 higher) he avoids spell-like abilities and uses the Trifaccia melee line. Below 150 hp he reverts to true form: flies, uses scorching ray, wall of fire, change size to shrink foes. At 50 hp or fewer he flees via invisibility back to Castle Korvosa (his bargain with Ileosa forbids plane shift escape); if he escapes, he may be re-encountered in the castle.

### EVENT 2: The Gray Mistress (CR 15) (line 14647)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** none
- **Encounter:** Sabina Merrin CR 14 (hp 193, stats p.454) mounted on Zarmangarof, male very old black dragon, CR 15
- **DCs:**
  - DC 25 Ride (standard action, Sabina): distract the dragon — flat-footed, -4 AC and attack rolls
  - DC 20 Ride (Sabina): fast dismount
  - Dragon gets opposed Sense Motive vs. Sabina's Ride result each time to realize her sabotage
- **Treasure:** none listed
- **Beats:**
  - The dragon rumor is true: Ileosa charmed black dragon Zarmangarof near the Sunken Queen and brought him to Castle Korvosa; Sabina got him as a mount (geas + charm monster compel him)
  - Sabina backstory: long-disillusioned with Ileosa (Trinia's execution, magic/torture indoctrination of Gray Maidens, blood veil, devil pacts, Togomor, murders); she secretly softened the Maidens' cruelty and protected Longacre prisoners; has realized Ileosa never loved her
  - Within half an hour of the PCs publicly defeating Yzahnum, Sabina flies the dragon over the city to draw the PCs to her; she never strays far from the castle
  - Her secret plan: use the PCs to kill the dragon — she can't do it alone; she steers it into bad positions via Ride
  - When the dragon turns on her, she dismounts and openly fights it alongside the PCs
  - Development: after the dragon dies she surrenders, confesses everything, asks for a chance at redemption; wants a safe place to talk (not the Dead Warrens — castle agents will hunt her)
  - If accepted: she provides castle intel (see Preliminary Research) and prefers rallying loyal city Gray Maidens to the rebellion rather than entering the castle; GM may send her along as muscle if the party needs help
  - Publicly defeating Zarmangarof = 2 RP; defeating/recruiting Sabina = 1 RP (3 RP if spared and recruited)
- **Tactics/morale:** Sabina — pre-combat drinks potion of barkskin; never attacks PCs; uses Ride to ground and hamper the dragon; once it turns on her, dismounts and goes all-in against it; after its defeat she casts aside arms and removes helm, surrendering. Zarmangarof — pre-casts mage armor, mirror image, shield; geas/charm force obedience to Sabina's Ride commands (no shared language: he speaks no Common, she no Draconic); prefers aerial magic/acid but lands as directed; below 100 hp he attacks Sabina in frustration, instantly taking -3 to every ability score from the geas; if the charm monster is dispelled he takes to the air and attacks PCs and Sabina alike; fights to the death.
