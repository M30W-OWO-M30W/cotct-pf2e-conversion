### E23 The Gizzard (EL 11) (line 8006)
- **Read-aloud anchor:** "This vast cavern stretches into the shadows, the true"
- **Encounter:** 4x Rajambari (variant mastodon elephant skeleton) CR 7 each
- **DCs:** DC 10 Acrobatics or Climb: move through the tangle of support timbers above the 10-ft mark
- **Treasure:** none
- **Beats:**
  - Power source for the labyrinth: four stone pillars are axles to the four great gears overhead; floor drops 30 ft below the eastern/northern ledge; ceiling braces form four 40-ft wooden disks; chains with bells hang from beams to pillars
  - Each pillar is attended by a chained rajambari (undead mastodon created by long-dead Vudrani necromantic methods); commandable by any Arkona but mindlessly loyal
  - When a lever is pulled above, bells ring, gears unlock, and the skeletons walk a quarter-circle around their pillar, rotating the labyrinth 90 degrees, then lock and go dormant
  - Timbers below 10 ft kept clear so the mastodons can work
- **Tactics/morale:** Passive unless creatures damage them or the Gizzard's structure, then all four defend it; each can move up to 15 ft from its pillar; their fighting shakes the rooms above (but rooms don't shift while gears are locked). If at least 2 rajambari are destroyed, the remainder can no longer rotate the labyrinth — configuration locks permanently. They fight until destroyed.
