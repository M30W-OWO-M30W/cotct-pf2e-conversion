The Scarwall Curse


Skill Checks, Saves, Other:
* Aura of Menace: Casting Detect Alignment [Evil] requires DC 34 Will Save vs. Stunned 4
* Dread: Resting requires a DC 26 Will save to gain the benefits of sleep.
* Phantasms: No conversion
* Soultrapping: Creatures that die in Scarwall must succeed a DC 31 Will save vs. the effects of bind soul into the Scarwall environs. Dispel Magic (7th level, counteract DC 31) allows a 1 round window to resurrect. For each week a living creature remains in Scarwall, a DC 28 Will save must be made to resist the effects of Soultrapping.
* Unhallow: Consecrate [Zon-Kuthon] (ritual 10th level, counteract DC 39), all of the castle’s inhabitants gain Protection [Good] (10th level, counteract DC 29) and +1 to saves vs. spells with the positive trait.
* PermEffect: AC: 1; ATK: 1, SAVE: 2; DMG: 1 evil; SAVE: 1 positive; SKILL: 1; PERC: 1
* Unhallow; AC: -1; ATK: -1, SAVE: -1; SKILL: -1; PERC: -1


Spiritual Doom


        Skill Checks, Saves, Other:
* Zellara: DC 31 Will Save vs. Soultrapping (Zellara has a +13, her deck’s bearer may make the save in her stead.)
* Magic Jar and Possession: Spells and effects that allow the soul to leave the body (possession, astral projection) require a DC 28 Will save vs. the effects of Soultrapping. The GM may roll a secret DC 31 [Tradition of the spell] check, which allows the PC to sense the danger and dismiss the casting as a free action. The spell slot is still used.
* Shaman Spirits: No conversion
* Speak with Dead and Speak with Haunt: Attempts to use Talking Corpse or similar spells that allow communication with the dead require a DC 27 Fortitude save vs. being permanently deafened. 
* Spiritualist Phantom: No conversion








Defeating Scarwall’s Curse


Skill Checks, Saves, Other:
* DC 31 Arcana/Occultism/Religion/Nature check to understand the complicated nature of Scarwall’s curse.
* One Spirit Anchor Defeated: The effects of Dread disappear and the +1 to saves vs. spells with the positive trait of Unhallow increases to +2 until all 4 spirit anchors have been destroyed.
* Two Spirit Anchors Defeated: The effects of Aura of Menace disappear..
* Three Spirit Anchors Defeated: No conversion.
* Four Spirit Anchors Defeated: Effects of Unhallow and all effects listed under Spiritual Doom disappear.
